# Claim rewards
1. Go to [RON staking](https://app.roninchain.com/staking) >
**My staking**.

2. Under **CLAIMABLE REWARDS**, select **Claim all**. This amount is
the reward emitted from your total stake to your validators prior to 00:00 UTC
today.
<img src={stepOne} width={1440} />

3. Select **Claim now**.
<img src={stepTwo} width={400} />

4. Confirm the transaction.
<img src={stepThree} width={400} />

After you claim the reward, the next claim is available at ~00:00 UTC 
the following day.

# Become a delegator
This page explains how to become a delegator and view your delegations on Ronin.

## Prerequisites
The only prerequisite is to have RON tokens in your Ronin Wallet.

## Delegate RON

1. Go to [RON staking](https://app.roninchain.com/staking) and select **Connect wallet**.

<img src={stepOne} width={1440} />

2. Switch to the **All validators** tab, pick a validator you want to delegate to, and then select **Delegate**.

<img src={stepTwo} width={1440} />

To learn more about a validator before making a decision, select the validator's name in the list to open the details page for that validator. You can also delegate from here.

<img src={validatorDetails} width={1440} />

3. Enter the amount of RON and select **Delegate** to initiate the transaction. To reveal your estimated monthly and daily earnings, click the arrow next to the estimated yearly rewards.

<img src={stepThree} width={400} />

4. Confirm the transaction.

<img src={stepFour} width={400} />

After the transaction is complete, the system displays a confirmation window.

<img src={transactionSuccess} width={400} />

## View your delegations
To view the list of validators that you delegated to, go to [RON staking](https://app.roninchain.com/staking) **> My staking**.

<img src={myStaking} width={1440} />

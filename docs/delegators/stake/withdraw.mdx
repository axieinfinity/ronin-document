# Withdraw stake
You can withdraw your stake as long as it's been three days since your last stake.

1. Go to [RON staking](https://app.roninchain.com/staking) **> My staking**.
2. Pick the validator to withdraw the stake from, expand the options, and then select **Withdraw stake**. 

<img src={stepOne} width={1440} />

3. Specify the amount of RON and click **Withdraw**. To withdraw your entire stake, choose **Max**.

<img src={stepTwo} width={400} />

4. Confirm the transaction.

<img src={stepThree} width={400} />

After the transaction is complete, the system displays a confirmation window.

<img src={transactionSuccess} width={400} />

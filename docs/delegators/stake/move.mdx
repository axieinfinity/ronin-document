# Move stake

1. Go to [RON staking](https://app.roninchain.com/staking) **> My staking**.
2. Pick the validator to move the stake from, expand the options, and then select **Move stake**. 

<img src={stepOne} width={1440} />

3. Specify the amount of RON and select **Move stake**. To move your entire stake out of this validator, choose **Max**, followed by **Move stake and undelegate**.

<img src={stepTwo} width={400} />

4. Confirm the transaction.

<img src={stepThree} width={400} />

After the transaction is complete, the system displays a confirmation window.

<img src={transactionSuccess} width={400} />
